CELLULAR RULEBOOK

1. SETUP - TRIANGLE FORMATION

3 Monsters arranged in a Triangle (1 Vanguard - 2 Wings).

  • Vanguard: At the front. Takes direct hits. Opponents MUST attack this position first (unless using bypass/AOE skills).
  • Wings: Provide support at the back. Protected by the Vanguard.
  • Vitality (Pellicle): Each Monster starts with 1 default Pellicle.

2. PELLICLE RULES (MEMBRANE SYSTEM)

  • Reinforce: Receive 2 Pellicles at the start of each turn to distribute (Max 2 Pellicles per turn).
  • Overload Threshold: Max 5 Pellicles per Monster. Receiving a 6th point -> AUTO-EXPLODE (OUT).
  • Chain Reaction: When a monster explodes, it deals 1 damage to adjacent allies.

3. TURN STRUCTURE (PHASES)

  1. Reinforce Phase: Receive 2 Pellicles and distribute them.
  2. Ability Phase: Activate support or passive abilities.
  3. Attack Phase: Choose only 1 Monster to attack.

4. THE LAST STAND (Final Monster)

  • Unstable Trait: Immune to Overload (can accumulate >5 Pellicles).
  • Energy: Receive 3 Pellicles during the Reinforce Phase.
  • Actions: Granted the right to Attack 2 times per turn.
