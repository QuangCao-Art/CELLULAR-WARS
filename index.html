CELLULAR RULEBOOK

1. SQUAD & FORMATION (3 CELLS)

  • Squad Size: 3 Monsters total (All Active).
  • Active Team: Vanguard (Front) + 2 Wings (Back).
  • Vanguard Rule: You have to destroy a Cell in Vanguard before you can attack Cells in Wings position (except some Trails can bypass this like Lydrosome).

2. PELLICLE RULES (MEMBRANE SYSTEM)

  • Reinforce: Receive 2 Pellicles at the start of each turn to distribute (Max 2 Pellicles per turn).
  • Overload Threshold: Max 5 Pellicles per Monster. Receiving a 6th point -> AUTO-EXPLODE (OUT).
  • Chain Reaction: When a monster explodes, it deals 1 damage to adjacent allies.

3. TURN STRUCTURE (PHASES)

  1. Reinforce Phase: Receive 2 Pellicles (3 if only 1 Monster left), distribute them.
  2. Action Phase: Activate Pellicle Trail if desired (1 action per turn).
  3. Attack Phase: Choose only 1 Monster to initiate an attack if desired (1 attack per turn).

4. GLOSSARY (TERMINOLOGY)

  • Cell: Official name for the organisms in combat.
  • Pellicle (P): Energy unit and membrane shield for Cells.
  • Vulnerable: Critical state at 0 Pellicles. One more hit causes Necrosis.
  • Necrosis: The "dead" state. Cells recover after resting in the Container.
  • Overload: Excessive energy (6+ Pellicles) causes Cells to explode.
  • Vanguard: The frontline position that takes direct hits.
  • Wings: Support positions protected by the Vanguard.
  • Reinforce Phase: Turn start where you distribute Pellicles.
  • Action Phase: Phase for activating Cell special abilities.
  • Attack Phase: Final phase where one Cell initiates an attack.
